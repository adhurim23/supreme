With the Swoosh-laden knitwear and tracksuits releasing at Western Supreme outposts today, we hit the streets of Paris to take a closer look at the collaborative collection. Styled in monochrome outfits against a rainy backdrop, the collection’s pops of red and blue come to life. A staple in Supreme's arsenal of repeating collaborations, this season's The North Face Collaboration came early in the seasonal rotation. The duo are set to release the Spring/Summer 2019 Supreme x Vans collection with the “Diamond Plate” all-over pattern across the Sk8-Hi Pro and Slip-On Pro models. The high-top features suede panels and canvas uppers for durability. The jacket, cargo vest, pants, backpack, and boonie hat are all rendered in a brushed-cotton 2C camo-OVD, while sports pants make use of the New Silk Light material you might recognize from Stone Island looks of the ’80s.
